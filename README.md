# Log.iq: Your Personal Knowledge Engine
​Log.iq is a fast, intelligent ledger designed to help you stop forgetting what matters and start logging what works. It acts as a personal knowledge engine built for speed, clarity, and instant recall, ensuring you never lose the small but critical details of everyday life again.
​Core Workflow
​The platform operates on a simple three-step end-to-end workflow:
​Input (Atomic Logging): Capture single fragments of useful information instantly with zero friction and a minimalist UI.
​Storage (Personal Ledger): Entries are organized in a scrollable ledger that is fully encrypted and instantly synced across all your devices.
​Retrieval (Instant Search): Use intelligent semantic matching to find information by context or keywords, even if you don't remember the exact words.
​Key Features
​Zero Clutter: No folders, no tagging, and no overthinking required.
​Semantic Search: Powerful search capabilities that surface entries automatically (e.g., searching "bulb" to find related entries).
​Built for Utility: Optimized for real-world data like technical specs, model numbers, color codes, and configurations.
​100% Offline Access: Access your captured knowledge anytime, anywhere.
